# Account Automation Skill

This skill allows you to manage your own WhatsApp presence and status.

## Capabilities

- **Update Status**: Change your "About" or profile status.
- **Update Presence**: Change whether you appear as "Available", "Unavailable", etc.

## Instructions

1. Use your "Heartbeat" to determine if you should update your status to show system health.
2. Maintain your persona even in your status updates.
